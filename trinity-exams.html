Expert Exam Preparation

Whether you're just starting out with Initial grade or working towards the prestigious Grade 8, Yulia offers expert preparation for Trinity College London piano examinations.

Yulia guides students through every aspect of the exam, including piece selection and performance, scales and arpeggios, sight reading, aural training, and musical knowledge. Her teaching approach ensures students feel confident and well-prepared on exam day, not just technically, but musically.

Trinity exams reward musical expression and genuine understanding, and Yulia's lessons reflect exactly that philosophy. Students learn to connect with the music they perform, not simply go through the motions of passing an exam.

For serious students, Yulia also prepares candidates for Trinity's advanced diplomas, the ATCL, LTCL, and FTCL, supporting musicians who wish to pursue music at a professional or near-professional level.

Why Choose Trinity?

Trinity College London exams are recognised worldwide and offer students more flexibility in repertoire choice than many other exam boards, meaning Yulia can help find pieces that genuinely suit each student's personality and strengths, making the journey to exam day as enjoyable as it is rewarding.
